# Daggerheart Overview
1. Improv Focused
2. Character & Story Driven
3. Required Materials
	1. Full RPG dice set (you may find you want more as you level up)
	2. 2d12 of different colors (one warm/one cool preferred)
	3. Something to physically represent your character on a map
	4. Some kind of tokens, roughly 7 - they are used for various things, and it'll be helpful to have them on hand.
4. Player Principles
	1. Be a fan of your character and their journey
	2. Spotlight your friends (share the space)
	3. Address the characters AND address the players!
	4. Build the world together
	5. Play to find out what happens
	6. Hold on gently
5. GM Principles
	1. Begin and end with the fiction
	2. Collaborate at all times, especially during conflict
	3. Fill the world with life, wonder, and danger
	4. Ask questions and incorporate answers
	5. Make every roll important
	6. Play to find out what happens
	7. Hold on gently
