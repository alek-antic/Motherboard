# Summary
The Tower Watch are a non-partisan group of skilled climbers who maintain the data wells around the [[Echo Vale]]. They are distributed across the valley, but tend to stick to the higher altitudes when they can. Many of the Watch are not comfortable in the lower valley.

The supplies needed to maintain these towers are dwindling at a rapid pace since the Remnant’s Fury, since the remnants are beginning to congregate at the data wells with increased frequency, leading to more damage as battles and data degradation are occurring more rapidly.
