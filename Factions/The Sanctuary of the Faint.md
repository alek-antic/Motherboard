# Summary
A secretive group of those who do not believe in the voice of the Motherboard, but rather a distributed collective of deities, known as the [[Faint Divinities]]. Similar to their decentralized theology, members tend to be spread throughout the [[Echo Vale|Vale]], traveling in groups of two at the most.

## Thematic Touchstones
1. Identity as Community
2. Belief
3. Progress
