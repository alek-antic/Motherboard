# Summary
An isolated walled city built into the remains of a giant skyscraper, elevated high above the floor of the [[Echo Vale]] by nature of a giant tree lifting the skyscraper out of the ground over thousands of years. Difficult to reach for most, Calcarus is lauded for its relative safety from wild remnants — at least until the [[Remnant’s Fury]] occurred. Prior, the citizens were heavily dependent on tame remnants for everyday tasks like trimming back the overgrowth, gathering water from the Vale below, and harvesting food.

However, they are now a community struggling to make ends meet. A supermajority of the tame remnants turned wild and had to be scrapped to keep the city safe. There aren’t enough hands to do the work previously done by the remnants, especially when those who can defend against future attacks are standing vigilant.

# Layout
The floors of the skyscraper have been mostly destroyed by the small forest growing upward through them. The citizens live in wooden buildings grafted to the outside of the tower. Communal spaces have been built on the interior of the building, supported by the large tree.

The data well in the city is located at the top of the skyscraper, making it the tallest data well in the Vale.
