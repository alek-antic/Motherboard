# Summary
An isolated walled city built into the remains of a giant skyscraper, located on a bluff high above the floor of the [[Echo Vale]]. Difficult to reach for most, Calcarus is lauded for its relative safety from wild remnants — at least until the [[Remnant’s Fury]] occurred. Prior, the citizens were heavily dependent on tame remnants for everyday tasks like trimming back the overgrowth, gathering water from the Vale below, and harvesting food.

However, they are now a community struggling to make ends meet. A supermajority of the tame remnants turned wild and had to be scrapped to keep the city safe. There aren’t enough hands to do the work previously done by the remnants, especially when those who can defend against future attacks are standing vigilant.
