Pronouns: He/they
Class: Rogue, Nightwalker
Heritage: Goblin Wanderborne

Originally from [[Copper Trove]], they eventually left and made their way to the [[Maytrader Caravan]] when they tried to steal <something/> from Foreman [[Grish Gavenox]]. When this was discovered, Grish’s union ran them out of town and is still in pursuit.

In leaving town, they were saddest to say goodbye to their sister [[Shiza Portar|Shiza]], who has a lot of debt to the [[Brokers]]. 

After becoming a member of the Maytrader Caravan, they joined with the [[Tony Express Trading Company]]
