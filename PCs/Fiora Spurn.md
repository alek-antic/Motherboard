Pronouns: She/her
Class: Seraph, Divine Wielder
Heritage: Wildborne Firbolg

Originally from [[Calcarus]], Fiora was assisted by an otherworldly power in striking down a group of Remnants that turned within the walled city. Since that day, she has been devoted to the [[Faint Divinities]], believing it was not the [[Motherboard]] that assisted in striking them down. For her dedication, Fiora was awarded with copper traces crawling through her armor, granting her divine powers.
