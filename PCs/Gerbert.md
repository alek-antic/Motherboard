Pronouns: He/him
Class: Ranger, Beastborne
Heritage: Slyborne Ribbit

Originally from [[Argent]], Gerbert was involved with the criminal underbelly located there. She was tasked with killing a rival mercenary, and almost failed. He no longer enters any body of water due to nearly drowning during this altercation. After this, she left Argent to travel, and found that [[Calcarus]] is her favorite region in the land. During his travels, he met Zing, a large cat that he befriended and now travels with.
