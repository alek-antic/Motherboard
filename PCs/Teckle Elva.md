Pronouns: He/him
Class: Warrior, Call of the Brave
Heritage: Human with mechanical goat hooves for improved climbing skill, Ridgeborne

Originally from [[Copper Trove]], he left for bluer skies in his youth. He became a member of the [[Tower Watch]], with his main passion is being up above the [[Echo Vale]]. He was trained by [[Viro Almscale]], both in the skill of repairing the data wells and how to fend off remnants.
He had a former companion named Uppy, which was a large hydraulic turtle used to reach higher places, but he turned feral when the Remnant’s Fury occurred.

He has an injured brother and father (unknown why).
