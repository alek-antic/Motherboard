She/They
Class: Druid, Warden of Renewal
Heritage: Underborne Simiah

From [[The Copper Trove]], Xuria worked as a medic in the mines. They grew an attachment to the remnants that worked there, and mended thoser who were injured in mining accidents. In the mines, they befriended Wireress, a remnant badger who had been caved in due to mining expeditions. Unfortunately, this bond did not last through the Remnant’s Fury, as it was corrupted and had to be killed.

Unbeknownst to her, she has an older sister [[Delta Foley]] who has been searching for Xuria after a tip from an anonymous informant. She has some sort of critical information to deliver to her.
